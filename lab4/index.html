Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-Solving

Challenge

The challenge of this lab is to practice psuedo code and problem solving. We were assigned to use bigger tasks and in turn break them down and go further in depth with them by putting them into smaller singular steps.

Problems

The challenge in this lab for me personally was to break down such the simple game that is rock paper scissors into much smaller steps. I also don't have much experience coding so it was a challenge for me to perform this function in that regard.

Reflection

This assignment was very helpful in terms of how to create an outline for coding and further than that problem solving as well. When doing the instructions for creating an online rock, paper, scissors game with short code, it was kind of hard to grasp an understanding of how to start it even though the game itself is very standard and very simple that basically anyone can know the rules and how to play.
